Spinach Stew

Description

Spinach and chickpea stew is a nutritious, plant-based meal packed with health benefits. Spinach is rich in vitamins A, C, and K, as well as iron and calcium, promoting strong bones and a healthy immune system. Chickpeas are a great source of plant protein and fiber, helping to support digestion and maintain energy levels. The stew is also made with heart-healthy olive oil and antioxidant-rich spices like cumin and coriander. This dish is low in calories but full of nutrients, making it a wholesome choice for a balanced, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic pressed or grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2 cans (480 grams) chickpeas or 3 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 500 grams vegetable stock
  • 500 grams spinach
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon in wedges
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts toasted

Steps

  1. Warm 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot, then add 1 large onion (chopped) and fry it for 3 minutes stirring often. Add 4 cloves garlic (pressed or grated), 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon ground coriander, and fry for 1 more minute.
  2. Add 2 cans (480 grams) chickpeas (drained), 500 grams vegetable stock, 500 grams spinach, 1 teaspoon salt, and 0.25 teaspoon black pepper. Stir and let simmer partly covered for 20 minutes.
  3. Taste and adjust for salt, then serve on boiled rice or next to warm pita bread topped with 2 tablespoons pine nuts lightly toasted on a pan 1 lemon cut into wedges.
  4. Optionally, you can top with yogurt tahini sauce, or plain, Greek, or non-dairy yogurt.
