Mediterranean Bowl

Description

The Mediterranean bowl offers a good source of protein, primarily from ingredients like falafel, hummus, and feta cheese. Falafel, made from chickpeas, is a plant-based protein packed with fiber. Hummus also provides protein from chickpeas, along with healthy fats from tahini. Feta cheese adds some extra protein, while also being a source of calcium. This combination makes the bowl a well-rounded, delicious, and protein-rich meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 handful mixed leaves
  • 1 tomato in wedges
  • 0.33 cucumber diced
  • 4 falafels
  • 0.5 pita bread warm
  • 30 grams feta cheese diced or crumbled
  • 6 olives
  • 3 tablespoons hummus
  • 1 tablespoon pickled red onions
  • 2 lemon wedges
  • 1 pinch parsley chopped
  • 3 tablespoons tahini sauce
